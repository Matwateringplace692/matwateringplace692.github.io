### How do I change the recording file size? 
You can modify the resolution and frame rate in the "Options" menu under the "Video" tab. Lowering these settings reduces the final file size on your disk.

### Does the software work without an internet connection? 
Yes. You only need the internet to perform the initial download and standard software updates. The recording features function offline.

### Can I capture a specific window? 
Yes. Click the "Selection" button at the bottom of the interface. You can draw a box over the specific area of the screen you want to record.
